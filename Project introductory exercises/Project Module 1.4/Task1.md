# Example case: Health centre with a single service point

Service point: a doctor

The queue: patients waiting to see the doctor. 

Arrival count: patients arriving to the health centre

Completed count: patients who have seen the doctor

Busy time: the time the doctor spends with each patient

Time: the total time the doctor spends with patients

Service point utilization: proportion of time the doctor is busy with patients

Throughput: the number of patients seen by the doctor per unit time

Average service time: the average time the doctor spends with each patient

Response time: the total time a patient spends in the system, from arrival to completion of service

Waiting time: the time a patient spends waiting in the queue before seeing the doctor
